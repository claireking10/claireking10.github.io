About

Hi, I’m Claire, a neuroscience and computer science double major at Trinity University with a strong interest in computational neuroscience and biophysics. My main research focus is virtual drug discovery for diseases involving protein aggregation, and I’m especially excited about how machine learning and gamification can optimize research and drug discovery. In the Cheng Lab, we work on discovering multi-targeted drugs that can disaggregate Tau protein in Alzheimer’s disease using a virtual drug discovery pipeline. After finishing my undergraduate degrees in May 2027, I plan to continue my training by joining a Ph.D. program.
